About The Series

Stranger Things follows a group of friends in Hawkins, Indiana as supernatural events, hidden experiments, and the dark dimension known as the Upside Down reshape their lives. What begins as a missing-kid mystery grows into a high-stakes battle against Vecna and forces beyond their world.

Across five seasons, the story combines horror, science fiction, and coming-of-age drama. The final chapter pushes every character toward one last confrontation to protect Hawkins.
